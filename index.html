The Etherstones

After a cataclysmic cosmic explosion, countless mysterious stones were scattered across the universe — each infused with ancient and untamed energy. These are no ordinary artifacts; they resonate with elemental forces, forgotten knowledge, and fragments of creation itself. For ages, they’ve remained hidden in the farthest reaches of space, lost across dimensions, worlds, and time. Legends whisper that those who manage to find and unite them will gain power beyond imagination — the ability to bend reality, alter fate, and awaken cosmic truths long buried. But such power comes with a price. Many have searched, few have returned. The shards are calling once more… Will you answer? The journey begins with a single stone.

Neural Implants

Ages have passed since the cosmic explosion scattered mysterious stones across the universe. Those who found them were forever changed. But it was only the beginning. As the final stones were retrieved, humanity evolved beyond belief. Scientists uncovered their secrets and created neural implants—sophisticated systems embedded in the brain that unlock hidden layers of the mind. Some gained heightened perception. Others could manipulate their emotions, their bodies, even time itself. But the implants are rare. Each one is unique. Some bond to specific DNA. Others awaken under celestial events. Legends speak of the Genesis Pulse—a moment when all implants unite into one consciousness. The one who completes the network may reshape reality itself. Now, as technology fuses with thought, one question remains: Who will activate it first? And what will they become?
