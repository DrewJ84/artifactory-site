The Etherstones

In the farthest reaches of the cosmos, beyond galaxies known to humankind, a cataclysm unfolded. Two ancient celestial forces collided — an explosion not of destruction, but of transformation. From the heart of this otherworldly detonation, fragments of raw, living energy crystallized into stones.

These were not ordinary minerals. Each shard pulsed with the essence of cosmic phenomena: gravity storms, solar fire, echoes of time itself. They became known as Etherstones — vessels of power, carrying fragments of the universe’s deepest secrets.

But the explosion did more than create. It tore through space, shattering dimensions, sending the Etherstones hurtling across the void. Through rifts unseen, they pierced Earth’s atmosphere like falling stars. Some embedded deep beneath mountains, some sank to the ocean floor, while others lay hidden in deserts, forests, and cities — silent, waiting to be discovered.

Legends whisper that those who find an Etherstone are forever changed. Each stone bonds with its finder, awakening a unique power — a gift, or a curse, depending on the heart that wields it. For centuries, they have remained concealed, mistaken for myths, guarded by shadows of history.

Now, one by one, the Etherstones resurface. Hunters, scholars, and wanderers seek them, driven by greed, hope, or destiny.

Step into the legend. Become a seeker of Etherstones, and claim the forces that lie beyond human imagination.

Neural Implants

Ages have passed since the cosmic explosion scattered mysterious stones across the universe. Those who found them were forever changed. But it was only the beginning. As the final stones were retrieved, humanity evolved beyond belief. Scientists uncovered their secrets and created neural implants—sophisticated systems embedded in the brain that unlock hidden layers of the mind. Some gained heightened perception. Others could manipulate their emotions, their bodies, even time itself. But the implants are rare. Each one is unique. Some bond to specific DNA. Others awaken under celestial events. Legends speak of the Genesis Pulse—a moment when all implants unite into one consciousness. The one who completes the network may reshape reality itself. Now, as technology fuses with thought, one question remains: Who will activate it first? And what will they become?
